Hi, I'm Cameron

I'm a junior games developer currently studying Games Programming at the University of Gloucestershire, but I'm originally from Oxford. Programming has been a hobby of mine since I was 13 years old, first starting with HTML and CSS to create basic websites. I swiftly moved onto software and then found my love for games development which incorporated my knowledge of C#, as well as my passion for video games. At the age of 12 I joined the Army Cadets and stayed with them for 5 years until I was 17. Cadets gave me the confidence I needed to work comfortably in a team, as well as on my own. Web and software development have remained a key part of my free-time, where I am consistently working to improve myself in these areas. Some other activities I enjoy in my free time is nature walking in the summer with friends, as well as playing video games. I am also an avid keeper of reptiles and creepy crawlies currently with a Cornsnake called Arlo, a Royal Python called Eric and a Rose Hair Tarantula called Fluffy. Fitting, right?
